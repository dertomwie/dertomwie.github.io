Bag of Scrambled Spells

This item allows its owner to cast new spells by creating runes of variying quality, that can be used to spell words.

Generating Runes

It works by creating new runes whenever a person that attuned to it uses a class feature, including spell casting. Your game master may decide to grant you additional rolls for other class-related activities. Every time runes are created, one is created for the source of the used feature (usually your class/subclass) and one for the feature itself (for example a spell's name or a feature's name).

Rune Quality

Runes are represented as one or multiple of the 26 letters from the latin alphabet with quality varying between -2 and +5. The quality of obtained runes decreases over the course of a day based on the amount of runes created and resets at dawn. Single-letter runes receive quality penalties based on the letter's frequency in the english language, while multi-letter runes receive no penalty.
With this in mind the quality of a rune is determined as 2d4 - 3 - accrued_malus - frequency_malus.

Rune Usage

You may use your turn to create whichever spellword you want with your obtained runes. (Your gamemaster might allow you to create a spellword in less than a full turn, e.g. in an action, or in more than a full turn if magic is more complicated to use in your setting.) You may describe an anticipated effect for your spellword, but it is ultimately up to your gamemaster to decide on the outcome of your spell. As a general guideline, consider the following recommendations:

  • If you use at least one rune with negative quality, your spell will likely have unintended side effects.
  • Intentional spelling mistakes may be counted as runes with negative quality.
  • A spellword of a summed quality of 8+ might have about the effect of a cantrip.
  • A spellword of a summed quality of 16+ should yield an effect as powerfull as a lower level spell a spellcaster of your level might cast.
  • A spellword of a summed quality of 24+ should yield an effect as powerfull as a higher level spell a caster of your level might cast.
  • A spellword of a summed quality of 32+ should yield an effect more powerfull than a spell a caster of your level would be able to cast.
These values may be adjusted, especially for short adventures.
